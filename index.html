WHAT WE DO

Our society continuously organizes several activities to promote cultural interactions and overall development of our department's students.

Workshops

Chemical Engineering requires the use of a variety of software. Some of the most important ones are MATLAB, COMSOL and ASPEN. Sessions are conducted by the society for CHE UGs and PGs to get used to these software

Intern Connect

Internships are of utmost importance during our college years. They provide exposure to the real world. One of the most valuable skills we gain from an internship is the ability to speak with people in a professional setting. They help us to learn more about ourselves and gain a competitive edge. The society conducts ConnectWithIntern sessions where students can get all their doubts cleared by some of the seniors of CHE department who have done internship in Core and Non-Core domains.

Symposiums

The society organizes symposiums with various esteemed and successful people belonging to the field of Chemical Engineering from the nation and abroad. Students learn a lot of things from the vast experience of the speakers.





Other Activities

These include organizing the fun-filled Happy Hours, publishing the Departmental Magazine ,T-Shirts etc. The society organizes the departmental freshers every year to welcome new students to the department. We also organize the farewell to facilitate the passing out batch.
